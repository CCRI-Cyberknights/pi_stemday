Welcome to WebGoat! If Juice Shop is the wild west, WebGoat is your interactive textbook. This application is specifically designed to teach you how web vulnerabilities work before asking you to exploit them.

üß† The Concept

WebGoat is broken down into specific lessons. Each lesson will explain a cybersecurity concept, show you the underlying code that makes it vulnerable, and then give you a sandbox to practice the attack right on the screen.

üéØ Your First Mission: Boot Camp

Because WebGoat tracks your individual progress through the lessons, you need to create an account. Don't worry, this database lives entirely on your local Raspberry Pi network, so nothing goes to the real internet.

Getting Started:

  1. Click the Register new user button on the login screen.
  2. Create a fake username and password (example: hacker / hacker).
  3. Once you log in, look at the menu on the left.
  4. Open the General section and complete the HTTP Basics lesson.
  5. Once you understand the basics, jump into the Injection section and try your hand at SQL Injection (intro)!
